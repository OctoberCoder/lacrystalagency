Who We Are

We are a Ushering and Protocol Agency established to provide ushering and protocol services to individuals and corporate bodies.

We are a young and specialized ushering and protocol firm, strategically located in today’s ever-changing socio-economic world, equipped to deliver innovative protocol and cutting-edge ushering services with class and style through our well-motivated and fetching staff.

What We Do

We provide everything you need to launch and grow a successful digital business.

We provide a wide range of services We render Ushering and Protocol services at Corporate Events, Weddings, Engagements, Product Activation, Advertisement of products or events, commercials, Print advertising etc

Welcoming Invitees

The ushers will provide assistance in welcoming the invitees to the nature of the event. Usher's task is to make the guest feel welcomed to the event with smiles and a positive atmosphere.

Greeting and Seating

The ushers will provide greet and direct invitees to seating areas in an efficient way. General help is also provided (depending on the client's request) in order for the event to flow superbly.

Registration

Our ushers are highly qualified and trustworthy to execute registration services. Before the event, our ushers are ready to meet with the client to ensure they are given the needed details and updates before hand. Our ushers are well trained in both registration, whether computerized or by hand.

Distribution

Ushers are regularly requested to distribute brochures, flyers, magazines and giveaways in certain events, as well as collect relevant details for the clients to get in touch with their customers, such as the customer's contact details.

Scanning

We offer our clients help in the scanning field, where certain data such as tickets are to be scanned. Our ushers are well trained and confident enough to take over the scanning area.

Surveys

La Crystal Ushers is proven to be highly reliable in the field of market research, to be specific the distribution, data collection and final data report of surveys.
